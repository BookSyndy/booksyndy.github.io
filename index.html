How

Getting started

Step 1:
Download the app

Get the app from the Google Play Store. It's entirely free to download and use!

Step 2:
Sign up

Enter a few quick details so we know who you are and what you're looking for.

Step 3:
Enjoy the app

You're all set! Buy or sell books or go over to the donate section to give them away.
